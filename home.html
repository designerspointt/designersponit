About us

Designer’s Point is a small, quality‑obsessed print studio focused on durable, great‑looking apparel for startups, creators, teams, and events.

Every order includes expert file checks and a clean digital mockup so expectations are crystal clear before ink hits fabric. We specialize in screen printing for bold, large runs and DTF/HTV for short runs or complex artwork—so the job fits the brief, not the other way around.

No product photos yet? No problem—mockups are accurate and realistic. When the first production series is complete, we’ll showcase real shots while keeping mockups for clarity.
